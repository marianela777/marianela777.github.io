About

A feminine, elegant bakery focused on meaningful details and unforgettable flavor.

Our Story

SOLI DEO CAKES began with one goal: create cakes that feel like a love letter. We design with a soft, romantic aesthetic—clean lines, delicate florals, and textures that photograph beautifully.

Every cake is made to order. We listen closely, sketch thoughtfully, and finish carefully—because your day deserves more than “just dessert.”
